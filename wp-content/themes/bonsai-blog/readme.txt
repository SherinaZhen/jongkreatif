== Description ==
Bonsai is simple theme for bloggers. Made especially for those who love nature, bonsai trees and fell in love with japanese maples.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Are there other photos at the top and can I use it on my blog? =
Yes. There are preapred other versions of photos at the top. Distributed on the same license. You can find it on theme website and just download it.
